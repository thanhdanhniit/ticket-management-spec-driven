# Teams (Roles Tab) - User Stories

These user stories were generated based on the "Team Roles" settings screenshots, covering the read state, inline additions, and inline editing workflows.

## Epic: Team Role & Capability Management

**As a** team administrator operating within a complex engineering hierarchy  
**I want** to securely view an explicit, granular breakdown of all the specific "Team Roles" definitively available within my active team  
**So that** I comprehensively understand precisely what systemic capabilities (Create, Read, Update, Delete) each defined tier inherently grants to its members  

**Acceptance Criteria**  
- Given I am deeply navigating the active Teams settings configuration  
- When I meticulously select the specific horizontal "Roles" tab  
- Then the system renders a stacked list of dedicated "Role Cards" (e.g., "Manage Team", "Admin")  
- And each rendered card explicitly visually lists exactly which localized sub-entities (e.g., Schedules, Services) are successfully bound to which distinct operational privileges (read, create, update, delete).

---

**As a** team administrator evaluating security baselines  
**I want** to comprehensively identify which foundational architectural roles are specifically locked and strictly immutable  
**So that** I conceptually understand the bedrock structural permissions defined by the overarching platform that I am legally prohibited from casually altering or outright deleting  

**Acceptance Criteria**  
- Given I am comprehensively auditing the Team Roles tab  
- When I observe a foundational system-managed role (e.g., "Manage Team")  
- Then the specific system clearly differentiates it visually from standard custom roles (e.g., applying a muted grey structural background overlay)  
- And the UI unequivocally prominently displays a small "Padlock" SVG securely adjacent to the role title  
- And the role functionally completely lacks an interactive dropdown actions menu entirely, securely preventing any attempt to violently edit or delete it.

---

**As a** team administrator designing specialized operational protocols  
**I want** to author an entirely new, deeply custom operational role and definitively map its capabilities to a highly precise sub-entity matrix  
**So that** I can confidently grant hyper-specific functional access to niche contractors or specialized shift workers without recklessly handing them blanket "Admin" structural privileges  

**Acceptance Criteria**  
- Given I am functionally assessing the Team Roles tab  
- When I deliberately click the fundamental "Add new team role" primary action button  
- Then a clean, massive embedded inline "New role" data matrix forcefully expands logically beneath the existing list  
- And I can freely establish a custom text title (e.g., "Read-Only Observer") logically in the "Name" input field  
- And I can interact with a rigorous, sprawling sub-entity checklist exclusively granting or explicitly withholding highly granular CRUD (create, read, update, delete) boolean variables uniformly across every single system feature  
- And securely clicking "Save" formally permanently commits the generated object tier correctly to the database backend.

---

**As a** team administrator reacting to changing structural internal engineering compliance requirements  
**I want** to organically modify the underlying specific capability matrix assigned structurally to an existing custom role  
**So that** I can surgically instantly instantly elevate or aggressively dynamically revoke privileges (like stopping them from exclusively deleting critical Schedules) entirely across the board for anyone definitively assigned to that specific role badge  

**Acceptance Criteria**  
- Given I forcefully identify a custom-authored overarching role card explicitly requiring foundational structural adjustment  
- When I methodically select the precise "Edit" option physically from its localized contextual kebab dropdown menu  
- Then the designated static card structurally dynamically gracefully morphs fundamentally into the interactive inline capabilities matrix structurally pre-populated perfectly with its exact current baseline checkbox states  
- When I selectively dynamically adjust the respective capabilities and execute "Save"  
- Then the modified matrix systematically overwrites the previous configuration matrix entirely securely and correctly re-collapses smoothly.

---

**As an** administrator executing fundamental platform housekeeping  
**I want** a dedicated isolated workflow strictly to forcefully explicitly obliterate obsolete or abandoned custom architectural team roles  
**So that** I can rapidly aggressively declutter the overarching authorization menu logically actively preventing structural confusion structurally when onboarding new engineers  

**Acceptance Criteria**  
- Given I am intentionally targeting an entirely redundant custom team role specifically for total systemic eradication  
- When I successfully purposefully click the bright red destructive "Delete" structural option precisely housed within the localized contextual kebab dropdown  
- Then the architectural system securely cleanly fully processes the explicit irreversible destructive removal correctly cleanly eliminating the operational framework from the team's hierarchy.
